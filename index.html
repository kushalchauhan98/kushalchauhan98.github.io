I am a Pre-Doctoral Researcher at Google Research India, where I work with Dr. Pradeep Shenoy and Dr. Sridharan Devarajan. Curently, I am interested in robust and interpretable AI methods with a focus in deep probabilistic modelling.

In the past, I've worked on multimodal representation learning for my Masters thesis, advised by Dr. Pramod Kumar Singh and Atri Mandal at IBM Research India. I've also spent two summers interning at IBM Research India and ShareChat working on NLP and creative text generation.

I graduated with a B.Tech and M.Tech in Indofmation Technology from ABV-IIITM, Gwalior, India in 2020.
